# Code of Conduct

We expect contributors and maintainers to collaborate respectfully and professionally.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, discuss ideas rigorously.
- No harassment, hate speech, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and technical.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block access for behavior that violates these standards.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, contact maintainers privately at:

- founders@galyarderlabs.app
